About

zFi is the first superdapp that integrates all of DeFi into a single domain.

Swap

zRouter is a fully on-chain DEX aggregator. Deterministic route solving with no off-chain APIs. Aggregates Uniswap V2/V3/V4, SushiSwap, Curve, zAMM, and Lido. Zero protocol fees.
